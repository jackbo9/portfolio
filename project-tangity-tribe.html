Tangity Tribe

Employee onboarding ritual for NTT Data / Tangity

Service Design Co-design Real-world Project Team Project

Context

A service designed to enhance employee wellbeing by creating a strong sense of community from the start of their journey with the company. Using the metaphor of the tribe, it offers a structured onboarding process with rituals as shared experiences. These rituals, spread across eleven milestones over a one-month journey, are crucial as they foster connection, belonging, and support, making new employees feel welcomed and integrated.

Problem

Fragmented onboarding, unclear guidance, weak early connections, and underutilized well-being programs in a multi-generational global team. Approach: Insight-driven reframing → participatory co-design workshops with ritual & community prototyping tools. Reframed HMW: How might we deliver clear onboarding guidance while building community from day one?
