Promap

Career development platform for construction workers

UX Design User Research School + Internship Real-world Project

Context

Promap is a digital platform designed to revolutionize workers' career development and enhance social recognition. By leveraging a comprehensive skill map, it supports individuals from the start of their careers, connecting them with the right mentors and providing opportunities to learn collaboratively with peers. Most importantly, Promap enables workers to effectively showcase their skills to potential employers, ensuring they receive the recognition and opportunities they deserve.
